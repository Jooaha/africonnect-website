About Us

ShonaPay is a global digital access platform connecting users to essential services including mobile connectivity, airtime transfers, and telehealth consultations through trusted partners.

Mission

To make essential digital services accessible, simple, and affordable for people everywhere, especially across Africa and global communities.

Vision

A fully connected world where geography is no longer a barrier to communication, healthcare, or digital access.
